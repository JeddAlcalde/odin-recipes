Adobo

Main Page

Description:

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb chicken thigh (skinless + boneless)
  • 1 small thinly sliced onion
  • 7 minced garlic cloves
  • 4 dried bay leaves
  • 1/2 cup light soy sauce
  • 1/8 cup rice vinegar
  • 1/2 tablespoon whole black peppercorns
  • 2 tablespoons of sugar
  • 2 tablespoons of water

Steps:

  1. Heat an oiled pan and add onions and cook until soft
  2. Add garlic to pan and cook until fragrant
  3. Push veggies to the side and sear chicken for 1 minute per side
  4. Stir all ingredients together then add soy sauce, vinegar, bay leaves, black peppercorns, sugar and water
  5. Bring those ingredients to a boil
  6. Reduce heat to simmer and cook uncovered for 20 minutes, then serve over rice
