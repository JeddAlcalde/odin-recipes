Lasagna

Main Page

Description:

Ingredients:

  • 9 Lasagna Noodles
  • 1 Tablespoon Olive Oil
  • 1 lb Ground Beef
  • 1 Diced Onion
  • Kosher Salt and Ground Black Pepper
  • 28oz of Crushed Tomatoes
  • 1 Tablespoon Italian Seasoning
  • 15 oz whole milk ricotta
  • 3.5 cups shredded mozzarella
  • 1 large beaten egg
  • .25 cup grated Parmesan
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley leaves

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F and lightly oil a 9x13 baking dish with oil
  2. Cook lasagna in a large pot of boiling water
  3. Heat olive oil in a pan and cook ground beef and onion while mixing seasoning
  4. Add in tomatoes to beef mixture
  5. Combine ricotta, mozzarella, and egg in a bowl
  6. Measure and put thin layers of cheese, sauce, then noodles on the baking sheet
  7. Place into oven for 35-45 minutes or until boiling
  8. Rest for 15 minutes
