Ramen

Main Page

Description:

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 3 teaspoons grated ginger
  • 4 teaspoons grated garlic
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 4 cups water
  • 1 oz dried shiitake mushrooms
  • 2 packages instant ramen(noodles only)
  • 1/2 cup chopped scallions
  • 2 cup chopped kale
  • 1 cup shredded carrots

Steps:

  1. Heat sesame oil and gry garlic and ginger for 2 minutes.
  2. Add broth and water, bring to simmer and add mushrooms and simmer for 10 minutes.
  3. Add instant noodles and simmer for 5 more minutes.
  4. Stir in carrots and kale.
  5. Top with anything you would like and serve.
